About

This is the personal blog and link list of me, Mikko.

Here you will find links or articles about things that pique my curiosity. Mostly things that have something to do with programming, web ops, start ups, gadgets or games.

I've been programming since the early 90s. First the Amiga with AMOS). Then PCs with Pascal and C. In early 2000 I started doing web development with PHP.

Last five years or so I've been programming projects with Python, Clojure and PHP. I'm available for contract work.

I'm currently working at OneMind Dogs.

My previous work history includes being a Senior Software Engineer at Gecko Landmarks and the CTO at YogaMe. Before those I was the Product Manager of a CMS at Poutapilvi web design.
