About

This is the personal blog and link list of me, Mikko.

Here you will find links or articles about things that pique my curiosity. Mostly things that have something to do with programming, web ops, start ups, gadgets or games.

I’ve been programming since the early 90s. First the Amiga with AMOS. Then PCs with Pascal and C. In early 2000 I started doing web development with PHP.

Last eight years or so I’ve been programming projects with Python. At the same time I’ve been really interested in learning the JVM ecosystem and doing functional programming with Clojure.

I’m currently consultant at New Things Company.

My previous work history includes being a Senior Solutions Architect at Anders Innovations, Tech Lead at OneMind Dogs, Senior Software Engineer at Gecko Landmarks and the CTO at YogaMe (Defunct). Before those I was the Product Manager of a CMS at Poutapilvi web design.

I’ve also implemented many different systems for various clients through my sole proprietorship.

If you have any comments or questions please feel free to contact me via email, skype or linkedin.
